Project idea-Doraemon Earphone

Overview

My product idea was called Doraemon earphones which is an animal translate earphones. Yes, you hear me right. Doraemon! The famous cat robot with the “Treasure bag”. Of course you may say I’m dreaming when I want to product a Doraemon. But my product idea is one of the objects in his bag. And can be realized—animals translate earphones. When people take it up, they can hear the animals’ voice through this Doraemon earphones. It’s not so easy to realize it in a few of years, because there are hundreds and thousands of kinds of animals in our world. So the first of our aims is to realize this work on our pet. Like dogs and cats. Doesn’t that sound nice when people can understand our pet?

Motivation - This should be a description of why the project will be interesting or useful.

The motivation of my product idea was appeared when I was young. I trust everyone watched the cartoon when they were young. Doraemon is my favourite cartoon, even when I am adult now. His can always get a lot of interest objects from his “Treasure bag” and these objects are so interested to me. The most one is the animal translate earphones. When he takes it up, he can understand the mean of the animals through their voice. It’s so interested to me. I like animals so much and I know it’s difficult to chat with them in voice. People usually use hand language instead of voice. But using this earphone, at least we can understand what animals thinking and what they want. It helps us to build a strong relationship between animals and us.

The features of the product is similar the normal translate earphone even the Bluetooth Wireless Headset like airpods that we use in daily. The most difference is this translate earphone contain a special chip that operate the translate work in the communication. It would identify the language of animals then send it to earphone. Now we can get it. People always say, “animals are our friends.” So communication is so important between friends. Or we usually do something wrong with a right heart. That’s why we should to product my Doraemon earphones to help us when we want to make real friends with animals. Once the Doraemon earphones were produced. The first place that it should service is the Animal research centre. For example, we all know panda is kind of the rare animals in the world. Even though our zoologists did a lot of work to keep the speed of the number of pandas decrease. It still less and less now. If animals disappear, human will be the second. That is not joke. Back to the topic, once the Doraemon was used in the research centre by zoologists, it would make a lot of works. For example, we can save much time to know animals’ habit, comfortable diet and how does the change of temperature will influence them.
